Interpretation Guide

Understanding Moods & Colors

The mood palette visually represents the core emotion of the dream interpretation. Here are the themes this prototype recognizes:

Freedom: Light, airy colors suggesting liberation and personal power.
Anxious: Muted, grey tones representing avoidance, confusion, or unresolved stress.
Calm: Cool, stable blues that reflect peace, emotional balance, and tranquility.
Insecure: Unsettling reds and oranges indicating vulnerability and self-doubt.
Optimistic: Bright, warm yellows associated with hope, fortune, and positive outlooks.
Mysterious: Deep, introspective purples for themes that are abstract or not clearly defined.

About This Application

How to Use

Navigate to the Interpreter tab, describe your dream, and receive a multi-faceted analysis. You can then save interesting interpretations to your Dream Log for the session.

About the "AI"

This application is a prototype developed for a university course project. The "AI" is a client-side simulation designed to demonstrate user interface and interaction design principles. It currently recognizes keywords to provide thematic results. It does not provide real psychological analysis.
